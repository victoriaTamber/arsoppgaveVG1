About me

How i got to this point

I have always had an interest in technology from an early age, but I have never gotten the opportunity to gain experience because of my problems attending school and bullying. In eighth grade I joined a coding club at Årvoll middle school. I have worked with micro bit, scratch, and Lego robots. In this club we joined a robot competition named Robot Lego League, we were competing with other coding clubs around eastern Norway. I had some small jobs with the club, and we introduced and taught children’s diverse types of technology. The coding club motivated me to attend school again at that time. Now I am 16 years old, and I am a high school student at Kuben.

The reason i have chosen IM at Kuben

I have chosen to attend IM because it has two education programs in one. Information technology and media production, because it gives me more options to choose what I would do in the future. I am a person that want to learn new things, my biggest curiosity at the moment and in the past is to learn Java script and other programs, but I have never had the opportunity to do it. But now I am learning at IM and in addition to that, I am very curious to learn more about cameras, picture editing and editing video in media production. One thing I have learned about myself while going at IM is that I focus on the task, and I do not play around.

What i have learned at IM

I have worked in Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Adobe Illustrator in Adobe Creative Cloud. In VSC (Visual Studio Code) I have learned HTML, CSS and a little bit Java Script. We have worked on network and webservers and we at IM have learned about complementary colours and how you can make a logo for websites. I have learned new things and I am excited to learn more. As a side project to this website, I am working on a website for MOKI. This is a project that is created by students that take the technology course and study at Kuben, and there I am making a website for their project that they are going to use. I have learned a lot at IM and i cant wait to learn more!
